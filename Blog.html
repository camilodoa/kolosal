PICK PATEK Interview

Kolosal Interview Series - EPISODE 4

Today on the Kolosal Interview Series, we are truly ecstatic at the opportunity to have Pittsburgh-based artist Pick Patek on the show. Citing Chance the Rapper and Kanye West as some of his greatest influences, Pick has been making music since the 5th grade and has been cultivating his sound ever since. After deciding to really commit to music his senior year of high school, Pick hasn't looked back. Moving from Abington, PA to Pittsburgh for college, this is when he started to gain a loyal fanbase. Pick has worked hard to make the large part of his art and video content by HIMSELF, and with an impeding move to LA, he is looking to go even harder in 2021. His latest single "Ready Baked", and latest album 'GHOSTS' are both available on Spotify and Apple Music, so make sure to go check them out.
